Aegis Vision is an end-to-end, multi-modal deep learning architectural framework designed to automate the identification and classification of traffic violations across Bengaluru's massive camera infrastructure.Manual enforcement of traffic laws is computationally and humanly unscalable. Passing unoptimized, continuous video feeds into heavy deep-learning networks results in massive cloud infrastructure costs and high false-positive rates due to environmental degradation (e.g., night glare, monsoons).Aegis Vision solves this through a Hierarchical Edge-to-Cloud Pipeline featuring our proprietary Smart Trigger Engine.Key Technical Innovations:Robustness Layer: Implements Zero-DCE and de-fogging CNNs at the ingestion point to normalize nighttime headlight glare and monsoon conditions before inference.Smart Trigger Engine (Cost Optimization): Heavy inference sequences (like ANPR/OCR using PaddleOCR) remain completely dormant. They are ONLY triggered if the primary violation classification heads return a confidence score of $> 0.85$.Multi-Head Classification: Utilizes dedicated sub-networks for specific infractions, including CNNs for helmet compliance, Instance Segmentation for triple riding, and DeepSORT temporal tracking over virtual polygons for red-light, wrong-side, and illegal parking violations.Cryptographic Admissibility: All generated evidence packages (raw image, annotated bounding boxes, and JSON metadata) are locked using SHA-256 hashing to ensure tamper-proof legal admissibility.
